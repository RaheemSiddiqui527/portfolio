Cafe Bloom Branding

Client Brief: A premium coffee shop wanted a warm, inviting brand identity that reflected their artisanal approach and cozy ambiance.

Challenge

The brand needed to differentiate itself from chain cafés while still appealing to a wide audience, from professionals to students.

Process

We explored earthy palettes, organic shapes, and hand-drawn elements. The process included logo sketches, digital mockups, and packaging prototypes.

Final Result

The final identity featured a minimalist logo with a stylized coffee bloom, warm colors, and consistent packaging design.

The project elevated the brand’s presence, leading to a 30% increase in customer engagement within the first quarter after launch.
