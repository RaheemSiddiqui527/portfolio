TechStart Logo Design

Client Brief: TechStart, a fast-growing technology startup, needed a modern, dynamic logo and identity system to reflect innovation and scalability.

Challenge

The brand needed to look cutting-edge while still being approachable to early-stage clients and investors. The design had to work across digital, print, and product mediums.

Process

We researched competitor logos, explored geometric shapes, and worked through several iterations focusing on simplicity and innovation. The process included mood boards, vector explorations, and mockups on digital platforms.

Final Result

The final logo used a futuristic yet simple wordmark with a unique icon symbolizing growth and connectivity. The visual system included a bold blue palette and modern sans-serif typography.

The new identity positioned TechStart as a professional, forward-thinking company, helping them attract investors and new clients.
